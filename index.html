Lionel Messi crowned The Best FIFA Men’s Player in Milan Beat fellow finalists Cristiano Ronaldo and Virgil van Dijk to the award Messi speaks to FIFA about the season past and his future with club and country Milan’s Teatro alla Scala acclaimed #TheBest on Monday evening. Lionel Messi collected The Best FIFA Men’s Player award for the first time, though it is the sixth time that he has been recognised by world football’s governing body as the player of the year. After beating fellow finalists Cristiano Ronaldo and Virgil van Dijk to the award, the 32-year-old Argentinian star shared his views of the evening with FIFA and looked back on his award-winning season.
